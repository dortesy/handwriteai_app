Thank you for using Text to Handwriting Converter AI ("HandwriteAI", "the App", "we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Process

To provide the core functionality of converting digital text into a handwritten style, the App processes the following information:

  • User-Provided Text: The text content you type or paste into the App's editor.
  • Handwriting Settings: The configuration options you select within the App, including chosen font, font size, color, line height, letter spacing, paragraph spacing, paper background, desk background, language settings, and selected effects (blur, shading, texture, shadow, rotation, margins, randomizations, etc.).

2. Purpose of Processing

We process the User-Provided Text and Handwriting Settings solely for the purpose of:

  • Generating the handwritten document images and/or PDF files according to your specifications via our backend server.
  • Displaying the text and applying formatting within the App's rich text editor interface.

3. Data Processing and Storage

  • Backend Processing: When you initiate the "Convert to Handwriting" action, the User-Provided Text and your selected Handwriting Settings are securely transmitted (using standard HTTPS encryption) to our backend server for processing. This processing is necessary to create the final output image or PDF file.
  • NO DATA STORAGE: We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not store your User-Provided Text or Handwriting Settings on our backend servers after the generation process is complete. The data is processed in real-time to generate the output, and it is not retained by us. The generated image/PDF file is sent back to your device for you to view and optionally save.
  • Local Storage: The App may store your most recent text content and settings locally on your device for your convenience (e.g., to resume editing). This data remains on your device unless you clear it or uninstall the App.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your User-Provided Text or Handwriting Settings to outside parties. The data is only sent to our backend server for the processing described in Section 3. We do not share this data with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data brokers.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures (including HTTPS for data transmission) to protect the information during its temporary processing on our backend. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13 (or the relevant age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Consent

By using our App, you consent to the processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website (https://handwriteai.app). You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when they are posted.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact@handwriteai.app
